Tv Shows




Suits

If you are interested in watching a movie or series that is connected about law. This right here is for you. This series doesn't only shows what the corporate law is, it also shows what can a person sacrifice for the ones they love. This series is about a hotshot attorney is considered the best closer in New York city. He ends up hiring a pothead genius without a law degree, while the genius was fleeing a failed drug deal. The show is about how they both along with many more significant characters handle various cases, simultaneously keeping their secret safe.




Lucifer

This is not an ordinary fantasy tv series. This series shows that even the baddest person can change for the one they love. The best part in this series is everytime there is a new investigation case that they are trying to solve, thos scenes are so entertaining. Lucifer is a television series that was on the FOX network but was taken over by Netflix after Lucifer's cancellation on FOX. It is based on a comic book series The Sandman, published by the DC Comics imprint Vertigo. The series was picked up on May 9th, 2015, and it is scheduled to air on TV in 2016.

The Half of It

This film is not like the ordinary love story that we often watch in the big screen. The plot of the story is very entertaining and there are many realizations that will hit you while watching this one. The film is a modern loose retelling of Cyrano de Bergerac in which teenage Ellie Chu begins writing love letters on behalf of the inarticulate Paul Munsky to his crush, Aster Flores.
