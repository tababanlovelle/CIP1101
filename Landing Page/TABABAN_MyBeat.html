My Beats

OPM

Filipino pop music, otherwise known as Original Pilipino Music or OPM, refers broadly to the pop music that first emerged in the Philippines in the early '70s and has since come to dominate radio airplay and karaoke playlists in the Filipino market and beyond.

Bata, Dahan-dahan by IV of Spades

The song is about giving warning for the youths of how dangerous the world is. Most of the kids nowadays wishes to grow up faster because they think that it is much easier, the song is telling them that it’s the other way around. Don’t rush things and let it flow normally because the world is not that beautiful as it seems.


Sino by Unique Salonga

Sino is a self-discovery song for anyone. Either you are exploring yourself or testing the waters of love, Sino is the best joyride song for a tranquil journey. It’s a typical feeling, falling in love and not knowing what to expect. However, even in the midst of confusion, we still find ourselves falling into unrequited love.


Minsan by Eraserheads

The song is about reminiscing their past memories with friends. The time where even though they have different perspective about life, they hope that their friends doesn’t forget about them even though they are now having their own life.






Pop Music

Pop music is the genre of popular music that produces the most hits. They have a good rhythm, a catchy melody, and are easy to remember and sing along to. They usually have a chorus that's repeated several times and two or more verses.

Before You Go by Lewis Capaldi

The song is about the aftermath of someone close committing suicide, including feeling like you are responsible for their death. Throughout the track, Lewis repeatedly questions whether he could have done something to prevent it.


All The Kids Are Depressed by Jeremy Zucker

The song is written to legitimize depression and acknowledge its existence to people. Jeremy Zucker stated that after his graduation, he moved into a house with his friends. Everyone living there took antidepressants except Jeremy. Since all his friends were depressed, he felt that the illness needed more attention and awareness worldwide.


Lowkey by Niki

The song is an anthem for clandestine romances and the thrill of keeping them under wraps. Accompanied by a bright and cheery visualizer, “lowkey” presents itself as a jubilant song anyone can jam to. Through a whimsical and silky melody, the artist begs her lover to keep their intoxicating dalliance a secret to everyone except the two of them.






KPop

Kpop is Korean popular music. It is mainstream music that originated from South Korea. The roots for K-Pop began from the 1950s, however, and have since then already been influenced a lot by different types of Western music and pop groups.

On The Ground by Rosé

The song is kind of about looking for answers in life for a purpose in life, and I feel like people especially these days, can relate to it, sometimes when you’re always kind of on that roll, you kind of forget to take care of what actually matters to you most, and it’s just a song that says everything we need is already within us.


Really by Blackpink

The song is about expressing your love for someone. The song says that if you really love someone you shouldn’t be afraid in expressing and showing it, sometimes words matter more than actions because actions has no certainty that both of you have the same understanding about a specific action.


You Never Know by Blackpink

The song is a motivational track about pursuing your dreams. Despite whatever the odds that are thrown your way, if you want to go for a goal, there is nothing stopping you from getting there, except only for yourself. The song also touches on how no one should be swayed by what others say to you, because they don’t know your story.
